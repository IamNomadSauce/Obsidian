Voltage
$$v=iR$$
Power
$$p=vi$$
$$w = \int_{t1} ^{t2} vi dt$$
$$p=i^2R$$ $$p=\frac{v^2}{R}$$
Sinusoid Signal
$$v=A\sin(\omega t + \phi)$$
$\omega$: frequency radians/second
$\phi$: Phase offset in radians

Root Mean Square (RMS)
	$v_{rms} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{T}\int_{t_1}^{t_1+T}v^2(t)dt}$
	$p = \frac{1}{T}\int_{t_1}^{t_1+T}\frac{v^2(t)}{R}dt$ 
	$p = \frac{1}{R}\frac{1}{T}\int_{t_1}^{t_1+T}v^2(t)dt$
	$p=\frac{1}{R}v_{rms}^2$
